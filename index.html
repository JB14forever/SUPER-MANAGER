About the Company

Super Manager is a robust and flexible portal solution built, which provides platform to Next-Gen Agents with customised features that makes them a perfect fit to be an Insurance Agent. ‘Agents First’ is our DNA. It drives everything we do. From the way we build our network to the way we connect agents to flexibility. We ensure every agent's performance, ability and their full potential will be enhanced with the use of our portal.

What we Offer

Flexible Managemnet

Various levels of priviliges can be configured to personalize data access, specific features or approval authority depending on the Agent's organisation.

User Authentication

When agents log into the platform for the first time, a verification email is sent to their respective entered email address to authenticate the user.

In-built Dashboard

The platform is equipped with a dashboard which will boost the agent performance, resulting in increased premium production.

Policy Information

Enables super fast policy information view with a simple one click process.
